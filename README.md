# pharr-pd-k9chase-game
Intaractive quiz game for PD deployment of the Pharr Way.

Play as Ammo, Shadow, Gunner, and Chase as they try to apprehend the nefarious Cat Nap Nabber Gang. The Cat Nabbers are trying to halt productivity and are attacking various departments across City Hall. The more questions you answer correct, the higher your score multiplier grows! Work as a team to stop all of the Cat Nabbers Cat Naps in City Hall. If you can't stop all the cats, the highest scoring player wins!
